How We Met

Our story began on a rainy Tuesday morning at a small coffee shop in downtown Portland. Emma was reading her favorite novel while James was sketching architectural plans at the next table. When Emma accidentally knocked over her coffee, James offered his napkins, and they've been inseparable ever since.

Three years of adventures, countless coffee dates, and one perfect proposal later, we're ready to say "I do" surrounded by the people we love most.
